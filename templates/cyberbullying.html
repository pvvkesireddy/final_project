Cyberbullying Awareness

This page provides information and resources to help understand and fight against cyberbullying.

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying involves the use of digital-communication tools (such as the internet and mobile phones) to make another person feel angry, sad, or scared, usually again and again.

Resources

  • StopBullying.gov - Information on cyberbullying and how to prevent it.
  • Cybersmile Foundation - A non-profit organization providing support for victims of cyberbullying and online hate campaigns.
